## Overview

Rob Kale helps you organize and manage all your project documentation in one place.

## Steps

1. Sign up at `https://robkale.com/register`
2. Confirm your email address
3. Log in to your dashboard
4. Click "New Project" to create a documentation space
5. Start adding pages and organizing content

That’s it! You’re ready to manage docs with Rob Kale.
