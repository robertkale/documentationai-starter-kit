---
title: Trouble Logging In to Rob Kale
description: Quickly resolve login difficulties.
---

## Symptoms

- Unable to access your Rob Kale account
- Error message: "Invalid email or password"

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    ````
    Go to https://robkale.com/forgot-password and follow the instructions.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your email">
    ````
    Ensure your account is activated and verify for confirmation emails.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    ````
    If issues persist, contact support@robkale.com.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
