## Quick Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Rob Kale">
    ````
    Go to https://robkale.com and log in to your account.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a new project">
    ````
    Click on "New Project" in your dashboard.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set project details">
    ````
    Enter a project name, description, and select a template (if needed).
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create documentation pages">
    ````
    Add your first page and begin organizing your documentation.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
