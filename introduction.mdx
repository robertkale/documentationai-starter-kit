## Overview

Undersigned.co streamlines e-signature workflows for teams handling PDFs and Word documents. You upload files, assign roles, route for signatures, and track progress with built-in reminders and audit trails. Designed for sales teams, operators, and founders, it reduces manual chasing and ensures compliance-ready records.

The platform supports drag-and-drop field placement, customizable templates, and a dashboard for real-time visibility. From initial upload to completion package delivery, Undersigned.co keeps deals moving efficiently.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Start by creating a free account at [undersigned.co/login](https://undersigned.co/login) to explore the dashboard.
</Callout>

## Quick Start

Get up and running in minutes with these steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">

    Visit [undersigned.co/login](https://undersigned.co/login) and create your account. No credit card required for the free tier.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Document" icon="upload">

    Drag and drop a PDF or DOCX file into the dashboard. Use the editor to place signature fields.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign & Send" icon="send">

    Add recipients, set routing order, and send the envelope. Enable reminders for faster completion.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Track Progress" icon="activity">

    Monitor status from the dashboard. Download the signed completion package once done.

  </Step>
</Steps>
